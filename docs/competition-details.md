 	2.	
Find the channel called #winter_challenge

 
 

 	3.	
Post there your workflow using Craft's MCP or API

 
 

 	4.	
Share it on any of your social media

 
 

 	5.	
Wait for the winner announcement! Or come up with other ideas and post them as well!

 
 

 	
 

 
 

 	
What is the timeline of the Winter Challenge?

 
 

 	
Submissions can be posted starting on the 27th of November at 00:01 CET and are accepted till 30th of December at 23:59 CET. The grand prize winner will be announced on 31st of December by 18:00 CET in the #winter_challenge channel and via email to the winner.

 
 

 	
 

 
 

 	
What are the prizes I can win?

 
 

 	
Besides the grand prize, we'll have monetary prizes for daily category winners in three categories:

 
 

 	•	
Simple hacks: Quick wins and easy setups, $500 per winner.

 
 

 	•	
Medium-complexity flows: More complex integrations, $1000 per winner.

 
 

 	•	
Complex scenarios: Difficult setups requiring coding, $1500 per winner.

 
 

 	
From December 8 to December 31, winners will be announced three times a week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 15:00 CET), with one winner per category each day. We'll review all submissions before each announcement to select the best in each category.

 
 

 	
 

 
 

 	
Are there any requirements for submissions?

 
 

 	
Just share your submission in #winter_challenge in Craft's community on Slack – it should include some usage of Craft's API or MCP functionality. Explain the problem you're solving and why it matters to you. Even the simplest hacks are accepted!

 
 

 	
It's important to have a published Craft doc with the setup instructions and, to spread the word, share it on any social media and link it to the submission.

 
 

 	
 

 
 

 	
Can I participate with a team?

 
 

 	
Of course! Feel free to team up with your friends.

 
 

 	
 

 
 

 	
Questions?

 
 

 	
If you have any other questions, just drop an email to winter_challenge@craft.do or ask us in Craft's Slack Community. There is a #winter_challenge_info channel you can use.
